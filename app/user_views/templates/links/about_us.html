About Us

Advancing gender equality through research, education, and community engagement.

Our Mission

To promote gender equality and women's empowerment through research, education, and community engagement. We strive to create an inclusive environment where all individuals can thrive regardless of gender.

  • Conducting gender-focused research and studies
  • Providing educational programs and resources
  • Advocating for gender equality policies

Our Vision

To be a leading center for gender research and advocacy, fostering a society where gender equality is not just an aspiration but a reality. We envision a world where everyone has equal opportunities and rights regardless of gender.

  • Creating a gender-responsive academic environment
  • Building partnerships for gender equality
  • Empowering future leaders for gender equality

Our Core Values

Equality

Promoting equal opportunities and treatment for all genders

Education

Fostering learning and awareness about gender issues

Community

Building inclusive and supportive environments
