About Us

Advancing gender equality through research, education, and advocacy

Our Mission

The Gender Research and Resource Center is dedicated to promoting gender equality and women's empowerment through research, education, and advocacy. We strive to create a more inclusive and equitable society for all.

Our Vision

To be a leading center for gender research and advocacy, driving positive change in society through evidence-based policies and programs.

Research Excellence

Conducting cutting-edge research on gender issues and their impact on society.

Education & Training

Providing comprehensive gender education and training programs for various stakeholders.

Advocacy & Policy

Influencing policy and practice through evidence-based advocacy.
